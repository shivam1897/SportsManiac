India The unbeaten champions of Kabaddi

India touched another milestone in 2004, when it hosted the first ever Kabaddi World Cup, in Mumbai. The country won the World Cup, as well. It has produced a number of talented Kabaddi players, so far, who have earned international recognition and brought laurels to the country. So far India is the unbeaten champion in Kabaddi World Cup.At a grand finale held at Guru Gobind Singh Multipurpose Stadium, the Indian men's team defeated Pakistan 45-42 for the fourth time, while the women team beat New Zealand 36-27 for the second time in a row. details...
